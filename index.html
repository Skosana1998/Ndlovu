How the journey started

Six days after the first free elections in 1994, Liesje and Hugo Tempelman started the construction of the Ndlovu Medical Centre in Elandsdoorn, a township in Moutse.

Programmes

Integrated programmes supporting people, families and communities.

Ndlovu Care Group’s work addresses healthcare, child and youth needs, water and sanitation, infrastructure support and practical skills development.

01

Child & Youth Development

Childcare programmes focus on supporting young people in their physical, mental, social and cultural development.

02

Water, Sanitation & Hygiene

Sustainable access to safe water, sanitation and hygiene remains one of the essential needs for healthy community life.

03

Infrastructure Support

Supporting safe and secure homes for child-headed households while maintaining organisational infrastructure.

04

Skills Development

Practical, hands-on training that equips individuals with the skills needed to access employment and build livelihoods.

Community Development

From healthcare access to youth empowerment, every programme is designed to build long-term impact.

The Ndlovu model brings together health, education, research, infrastructure and social support through an integrated approach rooted in community realities.

Focus Projects

Projects focused on skills, nutrition, hearing care and disability support.

These focus projects reflect the organisation’s broad impact across education, health and inclusive community care.

A

Vocational Training Centre

Youth unemployment continues to be a major crisis in South Africa, and the vocational school trains learners in Plumbing, Carpentry, Bricklaying, Electrical Work and Painting.

B

Nutritional

The first Ndlovu Nutritional Unit was established in 1996 and supports children and families affected by malnourishment.

C

Audiology

Ndlovu Wits Audiology provides comprehensive ear and hearing care through prevention, screening, diagnosis and active research.

D

Disability

Disability promotion focuses on early detection, effective treatment, rehabilitation and stronger inclusion for children living with disabilities.

Ndlovu Youth Choir

An outstanding professional ensemble with roots in after-school development.

Since its inception in 2009, the choir has profoundly affected the lives of the choristers and demonstrates the potential of any human being to achieve excellence.

I

Since 2009

What began as an after-school activity has evolved into a truly outstanding professional ensemble.

II

Excellence Beyond Background

The choir reflects how talent, discipline and opportunity can create excellence regardless of background or place of birth.

III

Human Potential

The choir stands as a symbol of what is possible when young people are given support, structure and belief.
